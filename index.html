SCOPING AND PLANNING

The Scoping and Planning pillar looks after planning, scope change, team capacity, client commitments, release schedule and roadmap publications.

The key processes are:

  • QRP - Quarterly Release Planning, where the main goal is to define the scope of work for the upcoming quarter and perform a retrospective review of the previous quarter (including predictability and capacity analysis).

  • PRB â€“ Product Review Board meets on demand to ensure overall transparency around product direction and major project decisions.

  • Deprecation process - Defines how we Deprecate / Remove functionality across R&D and the associated communications to our clients and stakeholders.

  • Client Commitment tracking process identifies any product gaps prior to contract signature to ensure there is capacity within the team to deliver any committed (DSL) items within the agreed timelines.

  • Release Schedule & Roadmap Publication.
